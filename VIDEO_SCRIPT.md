- Screen recording of the live app at **https://synq-web.vercel.app**

## FULL SCRIPT

---

### [0:00 – 0:20] THE PROBLEM — Set the stage

**Screen:** Desktop view of Synq at synq-web.vercel.app. Phone mockup visible in center.
Do NOT open the app yet. Keep it on the landing/home state.

> **VO:**
> "Here's a question nobody in productivity software has answered honestly:
> What do you do when you're too overwhelmed to even open your task manager?
>
> 85% of knowledge workers say they feel overwhelmed regularly.
> And when that happens — they don't open Notion. They don't open Todoist.
> They close their laptop. The thoughts stay stuck in their head.
> And the mental load just compounds.
>
> Every productivity app on the market assumes you're already functional.
> None of them meet you where you actually are."

---

### [0:20 – 0:45] THE PRODUCT INTRO — What Synq is

**Screen:** Tap the microphone button on the app. VoiceModal appears with waveform animation.

> **VO:**
> "Synq is different. It starts with a single question: how are you feeling?
> And then it listens.
>
> You don't fill in fields. You don't open a blank page.
> You just say what's on your mind — tasks, feelings, reminders — all at once.
> Like talking to someone who actually gets it."

---

### [0:45 – 1:15] THE DEMO — Voice capture in action

**Screen:** Show mic recording. Speak or display this transcript appearing live:

> *"Ugh, I'm feeling so overwhelmed right now. I need to buy groceries,
> finish the quarterly report, and call Mom — call her today at 5pm."*

**Screen:** Processing animation → Results panel slides in with 4 items:
- 😰 Overwhelmed (intensity 4/5)
- ✓ Buy groceries
- ✓ Finish quarterly report
- 🔔 Call Mom · Today 17:00

> **VO:**
> "One sentence. Four structured outputs.
>
> Synq's NLP engine detected the emotion — overwhelmed, logged with intensity.
> Extracted two tasks from the thought stream.
> And parsed the time reference — 5pm — turning it into a scheduled reminder
> for today at 17:00. Automatically.
>
> Zero form-filling. Zero friction. Under three seconds."

**Screen:** Tap "Save 4 items" → items animate into their respective tabs.

> **VO:**
> "Everything lands exactly where it belongs."

---

### [1:15 – 1:50] THE APP TOUR — Three tabs

#### Thoughts tab [1:15 – 1:25]
**Screen:** Thoughts tab — new items at top. Swipe one thought to complete it (checkmark animates).

> **VO:**
> "Your thoughts tab is your mind, organized.
> Swipe to complete. Filter by tag. Pull to refresh.
> No cognitive overhead — just your list, clean and actionable."

#### Emotions tab [1:25 – 1:38]
**Screen:** Emotions tab — show 7-day mood chart, then tap "Breathe" → breathing modal opens with 4-4-4-4 animation.

> **VO:**
> "The emotions screen tracks your mood over time.
> Seven days of data. Pattern recognition.
> And when you're in the moment — the box breathing exercise
> is one tap away. Four seconds in. Four hold. Four out. Four rest.
> Clinical-grade, built into the same app."

#### Reminders tab [1:38 – 1:50]
**Screen:** Reminders tab → toggle to calendar view → today's date has a dot → tap reminder to see "Call Mom · 17:00"

> **VO:**
> "Reminders with a full calendar view.
> Every entry linked back to the thought that created it.
> Nothing falls through the cracks."
